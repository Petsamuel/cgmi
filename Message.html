Above Only Devotional

Monday, August 17
Holy Spirit helps in Accessing our Divine Inheritance
Ephesians 1:13-14 (NLT), John16:15 (NlV)

And now you Gentiles have also heard the truth, the Good News that God saves you. And when you believed in Christ, he identified you as his own by giving you the Holy Spirit, whom he promised long ago. The Spirit is God’s guarantee that he will give us the inheritance he promised and that he has purchased us to be his own people. He did this so we would praise and glorify him. *All that belongs to the Father is mine. That is why I said the Spirit will receive from me what he will make known to you.”

There is no way that we can do without the Holy Spirit if we want to experience all of our divine inheritance in God. The Holy Spirit strengthens our conviction that we are God’s children (Rom.8:16) because this is what qualifies us to experience our divine inheritance. He is our guarantee that we will experience both the inheritance in us and the inheritance reserved in Heaven for us. We studied yesterday that for us to access God’s glorious inheritance, we need our eyes to be opened to them and to access God's glorious inheritance reserved in Heaven for us, we need to continue in the faith and allow the hope of this glorious inheritance to make us keep purifying ourselves. The Holy Spirit is the one who gives us the wisdom and revelation to know Christ and the inheritance God has placed in us, Eph.1:17. He is also the one that helps us remain focused on Christ and purifies us as we yield ourselves to Him, Rom. 8:4.

The Holy Spirit reveals our divine inheritance to us and helps us to experience them. Jesus said the Holy Spirit will glorify Him in our lives by taking all that belongs to Him and revealing them to us. The Holy Spirit does not only reveal the glory of Jesus to us, He also reveals the glory of Jesus in us. He is the One that transforms us from glory to glory into the image of Christ as we keep our focus on Christ, 2Cor. 3:18. Both the fruit (Gal. 5:22-23) and gifts of the Holy Spirit (1Cor. 12:7-11) are part of our divine inheritance. The Holy Spirit also quickens our mortal body, Rom.8:11. He manifests the resurrection power of Christ in our lives, Eph.1:19-20. The Bible says that the Holy Spirit can make us turn a wilderness into a fruitful field, Isa. 32:15. As you yield yourself to the Holy Spirit more, I see Him causing you to experience your divine inheritance more.

Prayer Focus:

Pray in the Spirit and yield yourself to the Holy Spirit. Receive greater manifestations of your divine inheritance in Christ.
