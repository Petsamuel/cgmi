Above Only Daily Devotional

By: Archbishop M E Benson Idahosa
Wednesday August 05
God Needs You Desperately
Ezekiel 22:30 ( NIV ), Isaiah 6:8 ( GNB ), Isaiah 59:16 , John 20 :21
I looked for someone among them who would build up the wall and stand before me in the gap on behalf of the land so I would not have to destroy it, but I found no one. Then I heard the Lord say "Whom shall I send? Who will be our messenger?" I answered, " I will go! Send me!" *And He saw that there was no man, and wondered that there was no Intercessor: therefore his arm brought salvation unto him; and His righteousness, it sustained Him. Then said Jesus... Peace be unto you: as my Father has sent me even so send I you.
The only way God can move or do anything on this earth , is through the help of a human being. The same applies to the devil. The reason for this is that God has put man in charge of what happens on earth, Psalms 115:16. Now, the only way that God can move or do anything through anyone is when that person willingly accept to yield to God and allow God move or flow through him or her. God's original intention was for man to be connected to Him so that there will be an agreement between what happens in Heaven and what happens on earth.

This plan was altered when Adam and Eve sinned and fell short of God's glory. They were disconnected from the life of God and there was a gap between what goes on in Heaven and what goes on in the earth. Death ( all evil ) began to reign over man and the earth, ( Romans 5:17a ) but God then sent Jesus to come down as a man to both relate with man effectively as well as die to rescue man from the power of the devil, Hebrews 2 :14-15. Jesus functioned on this earth as a human being. He slept, He ate, He was tired, He was tempted and He had to pray and yield Himself to God so He could do His will. God has now made us one with Jesus ( 1 John 4:17 , 1 Corinthians 6:17 ). We are the body of Christ, Ephesians 1:22-23. We are the flesh and bones of Jesus, Ephesians 5:30. You are His Ambassador, He wants to reconcile men, and situations back to Himself through you, 2 Corinthians 5:7-19. God has anointed you just as He anointed Jesus, that is why Jesus has sent you into the world just as the Father sent Him. Right now , God needs you desperately. He can not move and touch the lives of people without you. You are His eyes, mouth, hands, feet and heart. God wants you to be as passionate as Jesus in yielding yourself to Him ( Luke 22:42 ) just as Jesus was ( John 9:4, John 4:34 ).

Prayer :

I yield myself to you dear Lord. Come and save , touch and bless the lives of others through me today. Use me to pray , give, preach and bless others.
